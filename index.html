Moving towards renewable energy sources is the only way to go!

Renewable energy is the way of the future: not only are we saving the planet by going green, but we're also making the choice that makes most sense, in terms of fashion and good sense.

I have built this website mainly as a basic first step in learning web technologies, but also to persuade people that renewable is not more expensive, and it should be the younger generation's new "standard".

To put it in simple terms, clean energy sources like solar, and wind make the most sense because of the following reasons:

  1. They help reduce carbon emissions
  2. You can save money in the long-term
  3. They look good af!
